Typhoon Haiyan – Philippines

Hurricanes Manuel and Ingrid – Mexico Two separate storms overwhelmed western Mexico with rain in September, triggering widespread flooding and landslides. More than 200,000 people were affected in Guerrero state alone. In Acapulco, five feet of mud overtook vehicles and destroyed homes. World Vision staff provided families in the Xochistlahuaca and Santa Catarina River communities in Guerrero with food, blankets, and tarps. In the long term, we will provide clean water, sanitation kits, and construction materials to help families rebuild their homes. We will also operate Child-Friendly Spaces, where children have a safe place to learn, play, and receive counseling.
